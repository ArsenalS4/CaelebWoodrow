Technical Transition: Photoshop to Revit

Design Evolution

These technical drawings represent a crucial milestone in the Arsenal Studios project development: the transition from conceptual Photoshop-based design exploration to professional-grade Revit architectural documentation.

Phase 1: Conceptual Design (Photoshop)

Initial spatial concepts, visual mockups, and design ideation were developed using Photoshop, allowing for rapid visualization and creative exploration of the studio's architectural potential.

Phase 2: Technical Documentation (Revit)

Translation of creative concepts into precise architectural drawings using Revit, establishing accurate dimensions, structural elements, and technical specifications required for professional construction documentation.
